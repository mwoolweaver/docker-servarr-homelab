Welcome to Your MediArr Hub

Pi-hole

A network-wide DNS sinkhole that blocks advertisements and internet trackers for every device on your network without client-side software.

Sonarr

An automated PVR for TV shows that monitors RSS feeds for new episodes, manages your library, and sorts and renames downloaded files.

Radarr

Automates searching for, downloading, sorting, renaming, and managing your movie collection based on quality profiles you set.

Lidarr

An application that manages and automates the downloading, sorting, and renaming of music albums for your library, including quality upgrades.

Prowlarr

A central management and proxy tool that connects to various torrent and Usenet indexers and shares them with all your other apps, centralizing configuration.

qBittorrent

A free, open-source, and lightweight BitTorrent client used for peer-to-peer file sharing and downloading files via the BitTorrent protocol.

Jellyfin

A free and open-source media server that helps you organize, manage, and stream your personal collection of movies, TV shows, and music to any device.

Librespeed

A simple application that runs in a browser to test your internet connection speed.

NPM Admin

Nginx Proxy Manager provides an easy web interface to manage Nginx reverse proxy hosts, automate free SSL certificates using Let's Encrypt, and secure your services.
